poetry

dawn

mornings dipped in azure skies
wait for Moonlight's last goodbye
here thee tell thy story, Night
'fore sun goes o'er the rye

a-stationary-traveler

already i am forgetting myself -
the one emboldened,
reborn through quiet sights,
sworn to earth in a moment
on a northern shore
among the rocks and pines;
lay me down to sleep
until i can return
